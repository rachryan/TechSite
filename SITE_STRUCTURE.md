## Design Principles Applied

### Navigation Design
- **Simple**: 4 clear menu items, easy to understand at a glance
- **Logical flow**: Progresses from learning to exploring to taking action
- **Consistent**: Same navigation on every page
- **Mobile-friendly**: Hamburger menu on smaller screens

### Visual Design
- **Calm color palette**: Blues, greens, and warm accent colors
- **Subtle animations**: Gentle pulses, smooth transitions
- **White space**: Generous spacing for easy reading
- **Accessibility**: Good contrast, readable fonts, semantic HTML

### Content Strategy
- **Plain language**: No jargon or technical terms without explanation
- **Short paragraphs**: Easy to scan and digest
- **Clear headings**: Help visitors navigate content
- **Benefit-focused**: Always explaining "what's in it for you"

### Call-to-Action Strategy
- **Low-pressure**: "Explore," "Learn," "Browse" instead of "Buy" or "Sign up"
- **Multiple options**: Always provide choices
- **Clear next steps**: Visitors always know what they can do next
- **Repeated opportunities**: CTAs throughout the page, not just at top or bottom
