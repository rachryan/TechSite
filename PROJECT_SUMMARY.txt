TechTidy Website - Project Summary
======================================

COMPLETED: February 2, 2026

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Designed and implemented a modern, easy-to-navigate website that helps people feel curious 
and confident about improving how their technology works together. The site demonstrates 
how apps and tools can connect to simplify work and reduce frustration.

KEY REQUIREMENTS MET
--------------------
✅ Simple navigation - 4 clear, intuitive menu items
✅ Calm visuals - Soothing color palette with subtle animations
✅ Plain language - No jargon, benefit-focused content
✅ Subtle delight - Gentle animations and smooth interactions
✅ Clarity focus - Clear structure and messaging throughout
✅ Exploration - Multiple paths for different user journeys
✅ Low-pressure next steps - Inviting, not pushy CTAs

DELIVERABLES
